üß≠ Lesson Overview

Title: Refining Scientific Writing with AI: Ethical and Effective Practices
Duration: 2 hours
Delivery: Group-based computer practical via Quarto website
Focus: Prompt engineering, ethical AI use, iterative writing revision, collaboration

üéØ Learning Outcomes

By the end of the session, students will be able to:

  • Design and test effective prompts to improve their scientific writing.
  • Identify limitations, biases, and ethical considerations in AI-generated content.
  • Reflect critically on AI suggestions and apply human judgement to revisions.
  • Collaborate to iteratively revise writing using AI as a tool.

üîß Pre-class Preparation

For Students: - Bring a draft of a piece of scientific writing (e.g., from an assessment). - Ensure access to your Quarto class site and ChatGPT or a similar tool.

For Facilitator: - Load this practical session into the class Quarto site. - Provide sample prompts, ethical scenarios, and example outputs.

  • Class discussion: ‚ÄúWhat did AI get right or wrong?‚Äù
  • Pose key question: ‚ÄúCan AI help make your writing clearer without losing your voice?‚Äù

00:10‚Äì00:25 ‚Äî Activity 1: Prompt Design in Pairs

  • In pairs, students choose a paragraph from their own drafts.
  • Each pair writes two prompts:
    • Prompt 1: Make the paragraph more concise
    • Prompt 2: Adjust tone for a different audience (e.g., lay vs. academic)
  • Run both prompts through ChatGPT or similar and observe outputs.

**Try this:**

- [ ] Improved tone or clarity?
- [ ] Removed essential meaning?
- [ ] Introduced factual error?
:::
